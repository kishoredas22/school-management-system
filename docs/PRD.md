# PRD Summary

## Core Objective

Build a production-grade web-based School Management System that digitizes student management, teacher management, academic year control, attendance, fees, reporting, audit logging, and promotions.

## In Scope

- Student lifecycle management
- Teacher lifecycle and salary tracking
- Academic year lifecycle
- Student and teacher attendance
- Fee structures and immutable fee payments
- Reporting with drill-down behavior
- Audit logging
- Bulk promotion workflows
- Printable receipts and salary slips

## Out of Scope

- Parent portal
- Online payment gateway
- SMS notifications
- Mobile app
- Multi-school SaaS in phase 1
